# Eren Travel - Travel Management System
The Travel Management System is a digital system designed to connect travelers with travel agencies in a single environment. Instead of searching across multiple websites or contacting agencies individually, users can explore travel opportunities, compare offers, and book trips directly through the platform.
The system provides a space where travel agencies can publish travel packages and promotions while travellers can discover destinations and manage their travel plans. By centralizing these interactions, the platform simplifies trip planning and improves communication between agencies and potential customers.
## User Roles & Features
### 1. Traveler (Client)
- Search for travel packages based on destination, price, or travel dates
- View detailed information about travel offers
- Book travel packages directly through the platform
- Track past and current reservations
- Follow travel agencies to receive updates and promotions
- Leave reviews and ratings for services

### 2. Travel Agency
- Publish travel packages and promotional offers
- Update or remove travel listings
- Manage bookings made through the platform
- View customer reviews and feedback
- Track the performance of their travel offers

### 3. Platform Administrator
- Manage users and travel agencies
- Monitor activity across the system
- Review reported issues or inappropriate content
- Generate reports about bookings and platform usage

### 4. B2B Client (Business User)
- Register using travel agency details and tax ID to access B2B features
- Get verified to use the platform as a business client
- Browse travel packages with special B2B pricing
- Book travel packages at business rates
